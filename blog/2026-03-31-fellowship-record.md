The BeNiFIt MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowship ran from April 2024 to March 2026.

<!-- truncate -->

The website will continue to gather publications, software links, news, secondment activity, and follow on updates through the post fellowship maintenance period.

The main scientific record is organised around three objectives.

- In vivo biomarker sensing in vascular environments
- In body to on body communication using passive magnetic communication concepts
- Multi scale simulation for design space exploration and reuse

The project remains at modelling, simulation, and design analysis maturity. Follow on updates will be added when outputs become stable for public release.
