**Ethungshan Shitiri** carried out his academic secondment with **WBRG at NTNU**, hosted by **Prof. Ilangko Balasingham**, on magnetic field based communication for intrabody nanonetworks.

<!-- truncate -->

The collaboration focused on enabling communication between internal in vivo nanodevices and external wearable systems while reducing RF attenuation and meeting safety requirements.

During the secondment, the project advanced COMSOL based exploratory modelling of magnetic backscatter links and weak signal constraints.
