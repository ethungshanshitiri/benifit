BeNiFIt studies whether nanoscale sensing and nanoscale communication can be brought together in one framework for faster, less invasive biomarker readout.

The project scope connects three layers.

- The sensing layer in which biomarkers and nanomachines interact in blood
- The communication layer in which the sensing event changes the state of a miniature magnetic architecture
- The system layer in which an on-body gateway interprets the returned signal and the full setup can be explored in simulation

This integrated view is central to the project and will guide the structure of future outputs published on this site.
