## Software and simulation

BeNiFIt prepared a staged open science path for reusable simulation material. The simulator work connects adapted AcCoRD based blood vessel models, COMSOL based magnetic link simulations, MATLAB based design workflows, and example cases for future release.

- [Intra body simulator repository](https://github.com/ethungshanshitiri/intrabodySimulator)
- [BeNiFIt website repository](https://github.com/ethungshanshitiri/benifit)
